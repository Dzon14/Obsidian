# Magnetic field

### Magnetic field intensity
In vacuum, $\vec{B} = \mu_{0}\vec{H}$, where $\vec{H}$ is magnetic field intensity with the unit $[A/m]$.
$$\Rightarrow \oint\limits_{C}^{} \vec{H} \cdot \ d \vec{l} = I$$
